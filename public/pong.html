    Play

    1 Player Mode

    1 player mode is a game to 11 against the computer. The player uses the 'W' key to move up and the 'S' key to move down.

    2 Player Mode

    2 player mode is a game to 11 of two players on the same keyboard. Player 1 is on the left and uses the 'W' key to move up and the 'S' key to move down. Player 2 is on the right and uses the 'P' key to move up and the ';' key to move down.

    Infinite Mode

    Infinite mode is a 1 player game against the computer where there is no score limit. The player is given 3 lives and if the computer scores the player loses a life. The highest scores of infinite mode are posted on the 'Scores' section of this website.
