    • About Pong

    Pong is a table tennis themed arcade game. It was the first game that Atari developed, when engineer Allan Alcorn was given a training exercise of creating a game with two paddles, a ball, and a way to keep score. Alcorn created a high quality game, which surprised the founders of Atari and they published it.

    Pong was first created as a large arcade game similar to the one in the image to the left. Eventually it became avaible as a home console, and has become a first thought when thinking about retro videogames.
